// The Origin

We didn't start a brand.
We started a glitch.

GLITCHWEAR was born in 2024 from a simple frustration — Gen Z was tired of wearing the same algorithm-approved fits. We wanted something that felt like a corrupted file. Something that didn't load correctly. Something that stood out in the feed.

We took Y2K nostalgia, oversized silhouettes, and raw street energy — and mixed it with the glitchy, chaotic aesthetic of the early internet. The result is GLITCHWEAR. Every drop is limited. Every piece is a statement.
